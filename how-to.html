What Mosaic Is

Mosaic is an MLB companion app that helps you track games, watch live streams with MLB.TV, follow your favorite team, and review postgame recaps in one place. In short: baseball, but better.

Quick Start

  1. Open Mosaic and complete onboarding.
  2. Acknowledge the non-affiliation disclaimer.
  3. Optionally sign in to MLB.TV (or tap Skip for now and do it later in Settings).
  4. Set your favorite team and customize tabs.
  5. Start from Scores or Watch depending on whether you want game context or streams first.
MLB.TV credentials are stored locally in iOS Keychain on your device.

Navigation Map

Mosaic includes these main sections:

  • Scores: Date-based game discovery, live/upcoming/final sections.
  • Watch: Live games, highlights, Big Inning, multiview entry.
  • My Team: Favorite-team home, schedule, news, leaders.
  • League: League snapshot with standings, news, and leaders.
  • News: Filtered MLB news and article reading.
  • Standings: Division standings and team drill-down.
  • Leaders: Batting/pitching/fielding/running leaderboards.
  • Schedule: Full schedule view.
  • Settings: Team, MLB.TV auth, Live Activity, privacy preferences.

Scores and Game Detail

Using Scores

  • Swipe horizontally through dates (today and surrounding days).
  • Tap the calendar button to jump to any date.
  • Use Today to snap back quickly.
  • Pull to refresh the selected date.
  • Games are grouped as Live, Upcoming, and Completed.

Score Card Actions

  • Open full game detail by tapping a game card.
  • For live games with MLB.TV sign-in: use Watch Live.
  • Use Choose Broadcast to pick feed type when available.
  • Open Box Score from live/final game actions.
  • Track live activities from game-level controls.

Game States: Pregame, Live, Final

  • Pregame: Matchup info, probable starters, trends, weather, and team context.
  • Live: Real-time at-bat and score context with game progression.
  • Final: Postgame recap surfaces including story, moments, clips, and coverage.

Watch and Streaming

Watch Tab Basics

  • See Live Now games first.
  • Use Today's Lineup for non-live games.
  • Browse categorized highlight rails and play videos.
  • If Big Inning is currently airing, use the hero card to launch.

Streaming Flow

  1. Sign in to MLB.TV in onboarding or Settings.
  2. Open a live game from Scores or Watch.
  3. If multiple feeds exist, open the broadcast picker and choose one.
  4. Use the stream view tabs (Gamecast, Box Score, Play-by-Play, Live Games) while watching.

Multiview (Full Guide)

How to Enter Multiview

  • From Watch, tap the Multiview card (shown when signed in and at least two live games are available).
  • From a live stream, open context actions and choose a multiview option.

Builder Workflow

  1. Open Build Multiview.
  2. Select live games to fill slots (minimum 2 required).
  3. Tap a selected game again to expand feed pills and switch broadcast.
  4. Optionally add Big Inning if available.
  5. Tap Watch (n) when at least two slots are selected.

In-Grid Controls

  • Tap a tile to focus it (audio follows focused tile).
  • Long-press and drag tiles to swap positions.
  • Long-press without dragging to open the tile action menu.
  • Swipe up to open the stream editor sheet.
  • Use tile actions to fullscreen, replace, switch feed, add, or remove.
  • Toggle layout between Primary + thumbnails and Equal grid.

Long-Press Tile Actions (For Screenshots)

When you long-press a multiview tile, the menu can include these actions depending on current game/feed availability:

  • Watch Fullscreen
  • Exit to This Game
  • Switch Feed (when multiple live feeds exist)
  • Replace with Another Game
  • Add Another Game (if capacity allows)
  • Remove from Multiview
Screenshot placeholders:
  1. Long-press menu open on a focused tile.
  2. Switch Feed submenu/options.
  3. Replace/Add game picker flow.
  4. Resulting layout after a replace/remove action.

Limits and Behavior

  • Max streams: up to 4 on most widths, up to 8 on very wide iPad layouts.
  • If fewer than two stream URLs resolve at launch, multiview will not open.
  • Reducing to one stream exits multiview back to single-game flow.
  • In background, non-focused streams pause; focused stream is prioritized for continuity.

Multiview Troubleshooting

  • Multiview button missing: sign in to MLB.TV and ensure at least two live games are active.
  • Launch fails: retry after a refresh; one or more feeds may be temporarily unavailable.
  • Can't add more streams: you are at slot capacity for your current device width.

My Team, League, News, Standings, Leaders

My Team

  • Set a favorite team to unlock the personalized home surface.
  • Follow latest game context, schedule, team content, and quick links to roster views.
  1. Go to Settings and choose your favorite team.
  2. Open My Team for your team dashboard.
  3. Use team modules to open schedule, roster/player pages, and related news.

League

  • Use League for a broad MLB snapshot: games, standings, headlines, and leaders.
  1. Open League for an at-a-glance day overview.
  2. Tap into standings/news/leaders cards to jump to full sections.

News

  • Browse filtered MLB news, open article detail, and share links.
  • Some devices may support on-device AI summarization features where available.
  1. Open News and choose a filter chip/category.
  2. Tap an article card for full detail.
  3. Use share actions from the article view to send links.

Standings and Leaders

  • Standings shows division-level ranking and navigation into team context.
  • Leaders provides category-based leaderboards across batting, pitching, fielding, and running.
  1. Open Standings to scan division order and tap teams for deeper context.
  2. Open Leaders and switch between batting/pitching/fielding/running tabs.
  3. Select a leaderboard card to see full player rankings in that stat.

Settings and Personalization

  • Favorite Team: choose/change/clear your favorite team.
  • MLB.TV: sign in and sign out from Settings at any time.
  • Live Activity: control auto-start behavior for tracked games.
  • Privacy: control anonymous analytics sharing preference.
  • Tabs: customize active tabs and ordering from the More/Tab Editor flow.
  1. Open Settings for account and preference controls.
  2. Use Favorite Team to personalize app surfaces.
  3. Use MLB.TV section to sign in/out and restore streaming access.
  4. Use Live Activity and Privacy toggles to match your preferences.

Common Issues

  • No stream playback: verify MLB.TV entitlement and local network health.
  • Missing game updates: pull to refresh or revisit the date selector.
  • Unexpected tab layout: open tab customization and reset/reorder tabs.
  • Wrong team context: update favorite team in Settings.
