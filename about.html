Mosaic App Guide

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Mosaic is an MLB companion app for iPhone and iPad. It helps you follow live games, watch available MLB.tv streams, and keep up with your favorite team in one place.

What Mosaic does

  • Shows live scores and game state throughout the day.
  • Provides game detail views with lineup, context, and recent events.
  • Supports MLB.tv sign-in to watch eligible streams in-app.
  • Lets you personalize your experience around your favorite team.
  • Generates game recaps on supported devices with on-device AI.

Getting started

  1. Install Mosaic and open the app.
  2. Choose your favorite team during onboarding.
  3. Customize tabs and layout to match how you follow baseball.
  4. Optional: sign in with MLB.tv in Settings to enable streaming features.

Watching games with MLB.tv

Open a live game and choose a stream option when available. If a stream cannot play, the most common reasons are blackout restrictions, account entitlement, or temporary provider-side playback issues.

Your MLB.tv credentials are stored locally in iOS Keychain and used only to authenticate against MLB services from your device.

Recaps and on-device AI

On supported Apple devices, Mosaic can create game recaps using Apple Foundation Models. Recap generation runs entirely on-device, which means prompts and outputs are not sent to servers operated by Mosaic.

Troubleshooting

  • Stream not loading: confirm your MLB.tv subscription is active and retry after reopening the app.
  • Missing game content: pull to refresh and verify network connectivity.
  • Wrong team or tab layout: update preferences in app settings.
  • Sign-in issues: log out, then sign back in to refresh credentials.
