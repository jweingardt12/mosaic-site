Mosaic Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Mosaic (“the App”) is an MLB companion app published by Jason Weingardt (“we”, “us”). This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

What we collect

Email address and password (MLB.tv credentials). When you log in to MLB.tv through the App, your email and password are stored on your device in the iOS Keychain. These credentials are used only to authenticate against MLB’s servers on your behalf. They are never transmitted to, or stored on, any server operated by us.

Preferences. The App stores your favorite team, tab customization, and other UI preferences locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

What we do NOT collect

  • No tracking identifiers, advertising IDs, or device fingerprints.
  • No third-party analytics, advertising, or social-media SDKs.
  • No data is shared with third parties for advertising or marketing.
  • No cross-app or cross-website tracking.

Data you send to MLB

When you log in to MLB.tv through the App, your credentials and streaming-session requests go directly from your device to MLB Advanced Media’s servers. Their handling of that data is governed by MLB’s privacy policy. We do not mediate, inspect, or store this traffic beyond what the iOS Keychain stores locally on your device.

On-device AI

Mosaic uses Apple’s on-device Foundation Models framework to generate game recaps on supported devices. These AI-generated recaps are processed entirely on your device. No prompt or output is transmitted to external servers by us.

Data retention and deletion

All personal data (your MLB.tv credentials and preferences) lives on your device only. To delete it:

  • Log out of MLB.tv in the App’s Settings — this removes credentials from the Keychain.
  • Delete the App from your device — this removes all local data.

Your rights

Because we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is no account on our side to delete and no server-side data for us to export or correct. For any question about this policy, contact jasonw@duck.com.

Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If we update this policy, the “Last updated” date above will change. Material changes will also appear in the App’s release notes.
